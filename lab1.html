Tips and suggestions for playing with Syndra

  • Build
  • Runes

    Pros

  • She is good in almost any comp.
  • She has good CC with her slow and stun.
  • She has both burst and poke potencial.
  • Pretty good bullying in line.
  • Her true damade from her maxed W will really help with tanks.

    Cons

  • Pretty immobile besides her CC/flash for escaping.
  • She is all skill shot oriented, besides her ultimate.
  • If you miss your skillshots, you become extremely vulnerable.
  • She can be a little squishy.
